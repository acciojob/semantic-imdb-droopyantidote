Summary

"The Shawshank Redemption" is a compelling drama that tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongfully convicted of murder and sentenced to life in Shawshank State Penitentiary. Inside the prison, Andy forms unlikely friendships, including one with fellow inmate Red, as he seeks redemption and hope in a place filled with despair.

Actors

The movie features outstanding performances from actors such as Tim Robbins (Andy Dufresne), Morgan Freeman (Ellis "Red" Redding), and Bob Gunton (Warden Norton).
